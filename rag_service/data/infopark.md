# Infopark Kerala

## About Infopark

**Infopark Kerala** is one of India's leading Information Technology parks, providing world-class infrastructure and business support for IT/ITES companies. Established by the Government of Kerala, Infopark has become a thriving technology ecosystem that hosts global enterprises, startups, and innovation-driven businesses.

With modern office spaces, high-speed connectivity, sustainable infrastructure, and a skilled talent pool, Infopark serves as a preferred destination for technology companies seeking growth and operational excellence.
