# Lulu Group International

## About Lulu Group

Lulu Group International is a multinational conglomerate headquartered in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. The group operates hypermarkets, shopping malls, food processing facilities, logistics centers, and various retail businesses across the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and other international markets.

Founded by entrepreneur M. A. Yusuff Ali, Lulu Group has grown into one of the largest retail organizations in the Middle East.
