# Technopark Trivandrum

## About Technopark

Technopark is one of India's largest information technology parks, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. Established in 1990, it was India's first technology park and has grown into a major IT ecosystem supporting hundreds of companies and thousands of professionals.

---

## Key Highlights

* India's first IT park
* Located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala
* Home to over 450 technology companies
* Employs more than 75,000 professionals
* Spread across multiple development phases
* Focused on software development, IT services, AI, cloud computing, and digital transformation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Technopark?

Technopark is a government-supported technology park located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, providing infrastructure and support for IT and technology companies.

### Who can establish operations in Technopark?

Technology companies, startups, software firms, product companies, and innovation-driven organizations can establish operations within Technopark.

### Does Technopark support startups?

Yes. Technopark provides incubation facilities, mentorship, networking opportunities, and infrastructure support for startups.

### What industries are represented in Technopark?

Industries include software development, financial technology, healthcare technology, cloud services, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and enterprise software solutions.
